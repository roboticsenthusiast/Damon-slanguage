Have brackets, semicolons, etc. represent vocab "environments", theorem "environments", etc.
Smart highlighting
Use periods, commas, etc. to represent \items
Basically stripped down LaTeX specifically for notes

Rules: 
1. Doc starts out (optionally) with [font name, font size]
2. {Title} is enclosed with curly braces
3. *Major Concepts* are enclosed with asterisks
4. 'Minor concepts' are enclosed with ''
5. Generic text under major and minor concepts is formatted w/ tab spacing (like Python)
6. // always begins equations
7. Equations are highlighted green
8. Autocomplete is to be used for brackets, curly braces, *Major Concepts*, 'Minor Concepts'
9. Title is always bold black
10. Major concepts are bold blue
11. Minor concepts are normal blue
